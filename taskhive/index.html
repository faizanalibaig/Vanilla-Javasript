Welcome to Task - Hive

TaskHive is a lightweight and efficient task management web app designed to help you stay organized and productive. Built with Vanilla JavaScript, HTML, and CSS, it allows users to create, track, and manage tasks seamlessly.

Get Started

What we offer

Take your notes

Create, edit, and delete notes with ease. Store and access your notes anywhere you go. You can also create highlights and comments for easy reference.

Keep Record of work

Track your progress, set reminders, and see your tasks in a clear and organized manner. Remember that you will be notified when your tasks are due.

Stay focused

Prioritize your tasks, set time limits, and see your progress in real-time. If you have any questions, feel free to contact us at @gmail.com
